A list of my topics of interest is posted below.

Metaphysics

inter-level metaphysics (especially supervinience, realization, grounding, and emergence), material composition/mereology, objects (ordinary vs. extraordinary and the existence of fundamental individuals), properties (categorical vs. dispositional essentialism), laws of nature, indeterminacy (or vagueness), passage of time, nature of possible worlds, freedom of will

Philosophy of Mind

metaphysics of mind (especially physicalism and panpsychism), mental causation, intentionality (especially mental content), collective mentality (especially collective beliefs and intentions)

Philosophy of Language

metasemantics, descriptivism

Social Philosophy

ontology of social domains, social construction, metaphysics of race/gender, collective agency

Bioethics

biomedical ethics (especially philosophy of death and disability), feminist bioethics

Metaphilosophy

metaontology, metaphilosophical views (especially empericism and naturalism), conceptual engineering

20th Century Philosophy

the philosophy of David K. Lewis
